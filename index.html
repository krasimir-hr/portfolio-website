About me

I’m a web developer with a lifelong passion for building things on the web. Ever since I first started exploring how websites worked, I’ve been fascinated by the possibilities—how ideas can take shape online and reach people anywhere in the world. That curiosity grew into a drive to create meaningful, well-crafted applications that balance functionality with an enjoyable user experience.

One of my biggest strengths is problem-solving. I enjoy breaking down complex challenges into manageable steps and finding solutions that are both practical and elegant. Alongside my technical work, I bring years of experience working in collaborative team environments outside of programming. This has taught me how to communicate clearly, adapt to different work styles, and keep projects moving smoothly even when things get tricky.

Right now, my goal is to find my place in the web development world—a spot where I can contribute my skills, keep learning, and be part of something that makes a real impact. I’m excited about opportunities to work with creative teams, tackle interesting problems, and grow both as a developer and a teammate.
